MONTHLY PLAN
WEEKLY PROGRESSION
WEEK 1–2
BUILD
Learn the movements. Focus on tempo and form. Keep loads moderate — 70% of max.
WEEK 3–4
LOAD
Add 2.5–5kg to main lifts. Increase jump height or box height. Push intensity to 80–85%.
WEEK 5–6
PEAK
Test new maxes on squat and pull-up. Volume stays same — intensity peaks at 90%+.
WEEK 7
DELOAD
Drop sets by 1, reduce load 20%. Keep movements. Let the body absorb the work.
